*******GAME*******

The game is a first person shooter/builder like Fortnite. Players will be pitted 1v1 against each
other with a limited amount of materials and ammo. Players can build walls, ramps, floors and ceilings

*******ALPHA******

The alpha contains most of the building and shooting mechanics as well as movement and camera panning. 
Players can shoot, although there are no particle or other effects. The alpha does not contain the networking 
mechanics that will be implemented later.

*****CONTROLS*****

Movement:

WASD to move
Mouse movement to pan camera

Building:

E: Build Floor/Ceiling
R: Build Wall
F: Build Ramp

T: Shoot

Structures have health and can be broken by shooting them with "T". Structures take 6 shots to break.
